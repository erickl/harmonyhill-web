Harmony Hill is a luxurious villa tucked away in a traditional village in the heart of Bali. Perched upon a ridge, overlooking a gorgeous valley and luscious rice fields, we are a scenic 30 minute drive away from both the bustling town of Ubud in the South and the jawdropping volcanic landscapes of Mount Batur in the North.


Our aim is to offer you a unique opportunity to find sanctuary in nature, while enjoying the comfort of our beautiful villa and the thoughtful service of our team.

Good To Know

  • We are located in a lush jungle environment, which means many wonderous creatures made their home here long before we did. We don't have any poisonous bugs or lizzies here, just really colorful and oddly shaped ones. Just look at that little smiley face :)
  • The largest and furriest creatures you're likely to encounter are our four Bali kittens - all foundlings who found their way into our hearts
  • Harmony Hill is located about 20 minutes north of Ubud, at an elevation of 500m. That means we don't need aircon and can instead rely on natural ventilation to ensure you have a restful sleep.
  • We do our best to make Harmony Hill as sustainable as possible
    • We capture and filter rain water for toilets and showers
    • Our drinking water is filtered using ancient ceramic filtering techniques (find out more on Terra's website)
    • Our menu evolves with the seasons and nearly all our products are locally sourced
    • We are building a food garden, which provides jackfruit, durian, passion fruit, chaya (the miracle spinach tree) amongst other treasures
    • Any food scraps are composted in our garden
    • We are striving to make our villa plastic free
    We are always looking for ways to do better so we'd love to hear from you if you have suggestions or ideas ♥
