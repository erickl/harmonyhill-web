Harmony Hill is a luxurious villa tucked away in a traditional village in the heart of Bali. Perched upon a ridge, overlooking a gorgeous valley and luscious rice fields, we are a scenic 30 minute drive away from both the bustling town of Ubud in the South and the jawdropping volcanic landscapes of Mount Batur in the North.


Our aim is to offer you a unique opportunity to find sanctuary in nature, while enjoying the comfort of our beautiful villa and the thoughtful service of our team.

Nature's Kitchen

Our food is 100% plant-based. We are on a constant mission to curate for our guests the best locally-grown, healthful and delightful ingredients. Over the coming months after opening, we will be enriching the beautiful piece of land in our care into a luscious food garden, filled with tropical fruits, medicinal herbs and flavourful veggies for you to explore and enjoy.


Menu coming soon...


In addition to our menu and seasonal suggestions, our wonderful chef is entirely at your disposal during your stay and would be more than happy to try out new recipes upon request.

Stay In

Whether you want to relax on our wooden deck with a book and a tropical juice, pamper yourself with a traditional Balinese massage or jump into your private pool, we've got you covered.

Here's some ideas for a cosy day at Harmony Hill:
... Stroll around our tropical fruit garden and learn about the various plants
... Make your way down the garden to the most dreamy valley filled with rice paddies
... Bathe in a natural waterfall, just a 5 minute rice paddy walk from our doorstep
... Join us as we prepare the daily offerings or "canang"
... Play Catan with your friends and family (more board games to be added soon)
... Grab a book from our mini-library or bring your own
... Enjoy a floating breakfast in your private jungle pool
... Stretch out for a relaxing spa treatment of your choice (extra charge)
... Learn how to cook traditional Balinese food in a delicious vegan version (extra charge)
